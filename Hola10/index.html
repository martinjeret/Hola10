More About Me

I am an MChem graduate from the University of Edinburgh. I got into technology and growth hacking in the summer of 2016 when I started to build my first app - Backpack Chat. After several small projects I've started to offer product management, team building and digital marketing - Growth Hacking - as a service

Hmm... tell me more

One of the most common questions I get is "Why did you move away from chemistry" I love chemistry and still do.

My dream was to build a reasearch agency. I was working hard to establish myself as a chemist in university and managed to produce 3 papers and completed several projects in the Edinburgh University Young Researchers Society. However, in my penultimate year, when I was working in Singapore, I came to a better understanding of what my goal entails and decided to choose another path. Read the full story about that time in the blog.
